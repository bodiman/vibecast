About Tribe

Tribe is an intelligent framework analysis platform powered by advanced AI models. We help you understand complex data structures, relationships, and patterns in your frameworks.

  • Access to multiple AI models including GPT-4, Claude 3, and more
  • Deep analysis of graph-based frameworks with nodes and edges
  • Context-aware conversations about your selected frameworks
  • File upload support for compatible models
  • Real-time insights and recommendations

Our platform leverages the Lava API payment gateway to provide seamless access to cutting-edge AI models, ensuring you always have the best tools for your analysis needs.
